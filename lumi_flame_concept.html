Mascot Identity Sheet

Meet Lumi the Flame

A playful spirit of light — no species, no taxonomy, just a warm little flame with big eyes and a cheeky grin. Simple enough to work at any size, expressive enough to carry every emotion.

Character Identity

Lumi is a small flame spirit — abstract, warm, and endlessly expressive. The form is a single organic shape: a flame silhouette with no limbs in its resting state, just two big round eyes and a characteristic smile.

The design follows the same principle as the most successful modern mascots (Duolingo's Duo, Discord's Wumpus): a dead-simple base form that's infinitely extensible through expression changes and accessories.

Core personality: Cheeky but supportive. Not passive-aggressive like Duo — more of a warm companion who's genuinely excited when you read and gently sleepy when you don't. A bedside reading lamp with feelings.

Design Rationale

Why a flame over an animal? The flame is abstract enough to avoid species-specific baggage (no insect aversion, no owl clichés, no Mozilla confusion) while being distinctive enough to carry genuine personality. It's the same principle as Duolingo's success: the owl works not because it's an owl, but because the base form is dead simple and the expression system is rich. The flame achieves the same thing with an even simpler silhouette.

Why flat and bold rather than gradient-heavy? Your reference images show a single flat colour with no gradients on the body — just a solid, confident shape. This is critical for scalability. At 16px, gradients become muddy. A flat #FF6B4A flame with white eyes reads clearly at any size, on any background, in any context. It also prints well, embroiders well, and animates cheaply.

Why the accessory system? This is where the magic of the flame form really shines. The simple shape is a perfect "blank canvas" for layering accessories — glasses, hats, costumes — that can be unlocked through reading achievements. Kids see their flame wearing the crown they earned. Parents see a wizard hat after 100 books. This gamification layer ties directly into your existing badge and achievement system without requiring new UI architecture.

The "Lumi" connection: A flame is literally light. It's warmth. It's the thing you read by. It's also alive — it flickers, it dances, it grows brighter when fed. That's the perfect metaphor for a reading habit: the more you read, the brighter your flame burns. And the name "Lumi" — whether you trace it to Finnish snow or Latin light — works effortlessly alongside a flame character.
