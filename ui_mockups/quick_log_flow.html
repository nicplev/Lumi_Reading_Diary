🎨 Design Rationale

  • 3 Tap Maximum: Parents are busy. Reducing steps from 5+ to 3 significantly increases completion rates.
  • Pre-selected Defaults: 15 minutes is marked "Recommended" based on Victorian guidelines for Year 1-2.
  • Optional Details: Notes and photos are collapsed by default - never required but always available.
  • Instant Gratification: Celebration animation triggers immediately to reinforce the behaviour.
  • Streak Visibility: Showing the updated streak creates anticipation for maintaining the habit.
  • No Keyboard Required: The entire flow can be completed with taps only - no typing needed.
  • Smart Defaults: Book is pre-filled from current allocation. Duration defaults to teacher's target.
