🎨 Why This Flow Works

  • QR Code First: Teachers print QR codes on letters home. No typing email addresses or searching for schools - just scan and go.
  • Pre-filled Data: Child's name, class, teacher, and reading level are all pulled from school data. Parent just confirms.
  • Smart Defaults: 6:30 PM is pre-selected as the most popular reminder time. Most parents accept the default.
  • No Account Creation: Parents don't create passwords in this flow. They authenticate via the QR code link (Firebase Dynamic Links + email link auth).
  • Immediate Value: The "Tonight" card shows them exactly what to do before they even finish onboarding - reduces overwhelm.
  • Escape Hatches: "Enter code manually" and "Skip for now" give control without cluttering the happy path.
  • Zero Typing: The entire flow can be completed with taps only. Critical for parents doing this one-handed while holding a child.
