Mascot Concept Sheet

Meet Lumi the Firefly

A warm, minimal mascot that embodies the spirit of reading — a small creature that creates its own light in the dark, just like every reader finding illumination in a book.

Character Identity

Lumi is a small firefly who's a little sleepy (nocturnal, after all) but perks up the moment you open a book. Warm, encouraging, never pushy — Lumi's glow brightens with every page you read.

The design is grounded in your existing palette: the dark body (#2D3A4A) grounds the character with sophistication, while the amber glow connects to warmth and light. Translucent wings pick up your rose-pink and peach gradients.

Core quirk: Lumi is naturally drowsy — a nod to bedtime reading. The glow intensifies when engaged, dims when sleepy. Parents will relate.

Mood States

Lumi adapts emotionally across the app — each state maps to your existing mascot mood system

Default State

Happy

Resting glow, gentle smile, relaxed wings. The default state for navigation and idle screens.

Milestone / Streak

Celebrating

Glow bursts to rose-pink, star-sparkle eyes, body wiggles, confetti particles orbit. Used on success screens and milestones.

Nudge / Motivation

Encouraging

Bouncy motion, glow shifts to soft green, wide bright eyes, one arm raised. Appears when motivating continued reading.

Reading Log Flow

Reading

Holding a tiny book (in your lumi-peach), eyes cast down, glow steady and warm. Used during the log-reading flow.

Loading / Processing

Thinking

Gentle head tilt, one eye squinted, glow shifts to sky-blue, thought bubbles float up. Used for loading states.

Inactive / Empty State

Sleeping

Glow dims to cool grey, eyes closed, floating Z's, wings folded flat. Appears when no reading has been logged recently.

Onboarding / Welcome

Waving

One arm waving, bright eyes, standard amber glow. Used for onboarding, first-time greetings, and welcome-back screens.

Design Rationale

Why dark body + warm glow? A dark-bodied firefly grounds the character with maturity and sophistication — it doesn't read as "cartoon for kids." The warmth comes entirely from the glow, which is the emotional engine of the character. This contrast (dark form, warm light) mirrors the reading metaphor perfectly: illumination emerging from something quiet.

Why translucent wings? They pick up ambient colour from the glow state — amber normally, rose-pink when celebrating, green when encouraging. This connects the mascot to your existing colour system without adding new palette complexity.

Why the "sleepy" personality? Bedtime reading is the primary use case. A naturally drowsy firefly who wakes up for books is relatable for exhausted parents and charming for children. It also gives you a clear narrative arc for engagement: the more you read, the brighter Lumi glows.

Silhouette test: At 24×24px, the distinctive shape (round body + pointed glow below + antennae above) reads clearly. No other major app mascot shares this silhouette profile.
